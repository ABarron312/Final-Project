Nintendo History

Nintendo first started with craftsman Fusajiro Yamauchi who founded the company and after venturing into various lines of business and becoming a public company, Nintendo began producing toys in the 1960s and later video games. Nintendo has produced some of the most successful consoles in the video game industry.

For example:

  • Gameboy - 1989
  • Super Nintendo System - 1991
  • Nintendo DS - 2004
  • Wii - 2006
  • Nintendo Switch - 2017
  • Nintendo Switch 2 - 2025
