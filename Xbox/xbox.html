Xbox History

The first Xbox was released in November 15, 2001, in North America folowed by Australia, Europe and Japan in 2003. It competed with Sony's Playstation 2 and Nintendo's GameCube. It was first major console produced by an American company since the release of the Atari. It featured the ability to playback CD-ROMS and DvDs in addition to playing games.
